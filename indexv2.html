FAQs

What's the age requirement?

The show is 18+ to enter and 21+ to order drinks.

How do I find out the names of the comics who played when I was there?

We're glad you found a new favorite! We post stories on our Instagram each night with the lineup. Follow us at @atxcomedy

I'm running late, when do doors close?

Doors are open 15 minutes after the show starts. After 15 minutes your seats are forfeited to the waitlist. If you show up after 15 minutes and there are still seats available, then we can happily seat you.
