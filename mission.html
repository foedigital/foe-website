🎤 Meet the Founder

WM

Hey there! I'm Will Musselman, and I started Funny Over Everything because I wanted to do one simple thing: highlight the best comedians and shows here in Austin, TX.

Austin has an incredible comedy scene - from legendary clubs like Cap City and the Mothership to underground gems like Creek and the Cave and East Austin Comedy Club. But finding all these shows in one place? That was a nightmare. So I built this.

Whether you're a comedy fan looking for your next night out or a comedian trying to get your name out there, this site is for you.

🎯 What We're About

Our mission is simple: Create more resources for comedians and comedy fans to better engage in the world of stand-up comedy.

We believe comedy should be accessible. Fans should be able to easily find shows happening in their city. Comedians should have the tools to build their brand and reach new audiences. That's what we're building here.

  • Comprehensive listings of comedy shows across Austin venues
  • Free resources for fans to discover new comedians and shows
  • Tools and services to help comedians grow their online presence
  • A community hub for Austin's thriving comedy scene

🛠 How We Build

We follow a proven, phased development process to ensure your website is built right:

  • Phase 1 - Foundation: Set up the technical infrastructure with modern frameworks and responsive design
  • Phase 2 - Layout: Build navigation, headers, footers, and the core structure of your site
  • Phase 3 - Content: Create your homepage with hero sections, featured content, and calls to action
  • Phase 4 - Features: Add tour dates, ticket links, video embeds, and interactive elements
  • Phase 5 - Media: Integrate podcasts, clips, and platform links (Spotify, Apple, YouTube)
  • Phase 6 - About: Craft your bio, showcase credits, and press photos
  • Phase 7 - Contact: Set up contact forms, booking info, and email integration
  • Phase 8 - Testing: Cross-browser testing, mobile responsiveness, performance optimization
  • Phase 9 - Polish: Final touches - animations, SEO, favicon, and launch prep

Every phase is tested before moving forward. You get visibility into the process every step of the way.
