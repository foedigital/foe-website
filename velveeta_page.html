    DELIVERING

    LAUGHS SINCE 1988

    Welcome to The Velveeta Room, the longest-running comedy club in Austin. Tucked into an intimate space on 6th Street in the heart of downtown, we showcase a killer mix of veteran headliners and rising stars. The Austin comedy scene is flourishing, and The Velveeta Room has been at its center for almost four decades.

    Don't let the name fool you, at The Velveeta Room we keep it smart, sharp, and funny. You won’t find edgelord wannabes or tired shock-value acts here, just seasoned comics bringing the goods night after night. There's a reason we're the most trusted source for comedy in Austin, our standards are high because our audience deserves the best.

    With over 37 years under our belt, we’re more than just a comedy club, we're a bridge between 6th Street’s seedy past and its current comedy boom. In a constantly changing city, we welcome with open arms the new talent that has emerged here, while still proudly remaining a symbol of Old Austin. Come hang!
